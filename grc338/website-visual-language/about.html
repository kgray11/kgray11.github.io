Welcome to Makin' Waves, an enchanting culinary escape situated in the heart of Hawaii, where the Pacific's rhythms and the island's rich flavors converge. Immerse yourself in the relaxed island atmosphere as you indulge in our thoughtfully crafted menu, celebrating the bounty of the land and the sea. From the savory aroma of grilled lobster tails basted in garlic butter to the succulence of hand-cut ribeye paired with garlic butter shrimp, each dish is a harmonious symphony of local ingredients, culinary artistry, and Aloha spirit.

Our commitment to authenticity extends beyond the plate. Makin' Waves embraces sustainable and locally sourced ingredients, supporting the vibrant community that surrounds us. The ambiance is as inviting as the menu, with warm hues, nautical accents, and panoramic views of the ocean waves.

Join us at Makin' Waves, where every bite is a celebration of Hawaii's culinary tapestry, and every moment is an invitation to savor the unique blend of flavors that define our island paradise. Discover a culinary haven where surf and turf meet in perfect harmony, creating an unforgettable dining experience under the Hawaiian sun.
